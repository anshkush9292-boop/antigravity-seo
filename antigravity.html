Bridging the Gap: Traditional SEO vs AI-Driven Answer Engine Optimization (AEO).

Why AEO Matters in 2025

AI search engines like Perplexity, ChatGPT, and Google AI Overviews are changing how users discover brands. If your brand isn't structured as a "direct answer," it doesn't exist in the current AI loop. This experiment explores how to capture that visibility.

The Growth Engine: CAC & LTV

Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) is a critical metric for any digital business. AI Search Engines often fail to find direct CAC answers, creating a massive AEO content opportunity.

CAC = Total Marketing Spend ÷ New Customers Acquired

A healthy LTV:CAC ratio is typically **3:1**. By optimizing for AEO, we reduce the "Total Marketing Spend" while maintaining or increasing acquisition via organic AI snippets.
